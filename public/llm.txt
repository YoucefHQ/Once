# Once

> Love your work, not your distractions.

Once is a free, open-source browser extension that helps you stay productive by limiting visits to distracting websites to once per hour.

## How it works

- Add websites you find distracting (social media, news, etc.)
- Once allows your first visit each hour, then blocks revisits
- After an hour, the cycle resets

## Key details

- Browser: Chrome, Brave, and other Chromium-based browsers
- License: MIT
- Source code: https://github.com/YoucefHQ/Once
- Chrome Web Store: https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/once-block-distracting-we/cmkicojchpmgdakmdjfhjjibbfmfplep
- Website: https://onceforchrome.com
- Privacy: No data collection. All data stays local in your browser.
