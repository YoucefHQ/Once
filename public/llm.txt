# Once

> Stop wasting time on distracting websites.

Once is a free Chrome extension that limits time on distracting websites by letting you visit them only once per hour.

## How it works

1. Install Once for free and add the websites you find distracting like X, Reddit, or Instagram.
2. Once allows you to visit those websites once per hour and blocks you until the hour resets.
3. The timer starts when you close the tab of one of these websites and resets automatically every hour.
