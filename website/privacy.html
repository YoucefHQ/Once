Privacy Policy

We don't collect any data

Once does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services.

Everything stays on your device

Your list of blocked websites and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

No accounts, no sign-ups

Once doesn't require an account. There's nothing to sign up for and no credentials to manage.

Open source

Don't take our word for it. Once is open source — you can read every line of code yourself.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy, open an issue on GitHub.
